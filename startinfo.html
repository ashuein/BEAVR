A Browser-based tool for the Exploration And Visualization of RNA-seq data


General steps to follow:

  1. Load the read count table file
      A csv or txt file containing your raw read count data. ie. the output file from your aligner such as STAR.
  2. Load the sample treatment matrix file
      A csv or txt file containing additional information about the samples in your experiment.
  3. Click the Settings tab
      Specify some settings for your experiment like the control and treatment samples and false discovery rate
  4. Click the Gene Table tab
      Once you click this tab, differential gene expression will be calculated automatically. This may take a while depending on your computer.
  5. Explore your data
      Once differential gene expression is calculated, you can use the other tabs to explore and visualize your data.

Check the Help tab for more info.
